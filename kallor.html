  2. Jones Jr, J. B. (2016). Hydroponics: a practical guide for the soilless grower. CRC press.
  3. Roberto, K. (2003). How-to Hydroponics. Futuregarden Press.
  4. Benton Jones, J. (2014). Complete Guide for Growing Plants Hydroponically. CRC Press.
  5. Raviv, M., & Lieth, J. H. (Eds.). (2007). Soilless Culture: Theory and Practice. Elsevier.
  6. Savvas, D., & Passam, H. (Eds.). (2002). Hydroponic Production of Vegetables and Ornamentals. Embryo Publications.
  7. Jensen, M. H., & Collins, W. L. (1985). Hydroponic vegetable production. Horticultural Reviews, 7, 483-558.
  8. Trejo-Téllez, L. I., & Gómez-Merino, F. C. (2012). Nutrient solutions for hydroponic systems. Hydroponics-A Standard Methodology for Plant Biological Researches, 1-22.
